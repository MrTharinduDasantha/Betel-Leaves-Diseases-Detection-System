Betel Leaf Diseases Detection System

Project Overview

The Betel Leaf Diseases Detection System is a comprehensive platform designed to assist farmers and agricultural officers in the management of betel leaf cultivation. Users can register as either an admin (Agricultural Officer) or a user (Farmer) to access various features of the system.

Our system allows users to identify betel leaf diseases through our advanced AI model, providing detailed solutions including Disease Identification, Symptoms, Treatment Methods, and Preventative Measures. Information about maintaining healthy betel cultivation is also available, with administrators having the ability to update these solutions as needed.

Beyond disease identification, our platform offers additional features such as a Community Forum where farmers can share cultivation challenges and experiences, a Consult Officer section for direct communication with agricultural experts, and a Cultivation Guide with comprehensive instructions for betel farming.

Dataset Information

Our disease detection model is trained on a specialized dataset of betel leaf images, categorizing them into four distinct conditions: healthy, dried, afflicted by bacterial disease, and suffering from fungal brown spot disease. Captured in Camperhat, Raipur, Lakshmipur, Bangladesh, this collection demonstrates a meticulous approach to data gathering for disease identification.

The dataset contains 3589 images organized into Original (1000 images) and Augmented (2589 images) folders. The Augmented folder is further divided into training (2388 images) and testing (201 images) subsets, designed to support both deep learning and machine learning algorithms for precise classification of betel leaf health conditions.
